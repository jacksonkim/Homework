How to repair a jumpring slip

As stated on the Maintenance Hatch page, the metals that I work with are a bit malleable. When tugged on or worn often, sometimes the rings at the ends of the necklaces, bracelets, or on the earrings will stretch to a point that allows the article of jewelry to fall off.
