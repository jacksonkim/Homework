Once Winona had gone, Protoman lie on the slab for an indeterminate amount of time.
The backup had not been completed yet, so he allowed himself to stroke Carly’s
shoulders absently with his only hand. He only ever had one hand, but the knowledge
that he no longer had his gun either kept replaying in his mind between flashes of
memories as they were transferred. He was incomplete, moreso than usual. He had seen
humans in Wily’s secret dungeons with similar injuries.
